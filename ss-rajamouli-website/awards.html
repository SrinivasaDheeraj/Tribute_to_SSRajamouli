Awards & Achievements

  • Padma Shri (2016) – The Padma Shri is India’s fourth-highest civilian award, conferred by the Government of India in recognition of Rajamouli’s exceptional contribution to Indian cinema and his role in elevating the Telugu film industry to global prominence.
  • National Film Awards – Rajamouli has won multiple National Film Awards, India’s most prestigious film honors, for his excellence in direction and storytelling, particularly for his ability to craft visually stunning and emotionally resonant narratives.
  • Oscar & Golden Globe (2023) – Rajamouli’s RRR made history by winning the Academy Award and Golden Globe for Best Original Song for "Naatu Naatu," marking a monumental achievement for Indian cinema on the global stage.
  • Filmfare Awards – Rajamouli has received numerous Filmfare Awards, including Best Director and Best Film, for his exceptional filmmaking and the widespread critical and commercial success of his movies. These awards highlight his dominance in Indian cinema and his ability to captivate audiences with his visionary storytelling.
